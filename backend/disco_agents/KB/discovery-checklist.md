# Discovery Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist defines the information required to write a comprehensive PRD for any strategic initiative. Use it to plan discovery sessions and track coverage.

---

## 1. Problem Definition

### Current State
- [ ] How does the process work today?
- [ ] Who are the key actors involved?
- [ ] What systems/tools are currently used?
- [ ] What data exists and where does it live?
- [ ] What's the current workflow end-to-end?

### Pain Points
- [ ] What's broken or frustrating about the current state?
- [ ] Where do delays or bottlenecks occur?
- [ ] What manual work could be reduced?
- [ ] What errors or inconsistencies occur?
- [ ] What visibility is lacking?

### Impact of Problems
- [ ] How much time is lost to current inefficiencies?
- [ ] What's the business cost of the status quo?
- [ ] What risks exist if nothing changes?
- [ ] What opportunities are being missed?

---

## 2. Stakeholder Perspectives

### Primary Users
- [ ] Who will use the solution daily?
- [ ] What are their goals and success criteria?
- [ ] What constraints do they operate under?
- [ ] What's their technical sophistication?

### Secondary Stakeholders
- [ ] Who benefits indirectly from the solution?
- [ ] Who provides input or approvals?
- [ ] Who will maintain/support the solution?
- [ ] Who has budget authority?

### Conflicting Needs
- [ ] Where do stakeholder priorities differ?
- [ ] What trade-offs need to be made?
- [ ] Who is the tiebreaker for conflicts?

---

## 3. Requirements

### Functional Requirements
- [ ] What must the solution do? (core capabilities)
- [ ] What workflows must be supported?
- [ ] What inputs and outputs are needed?
- [ ] What integrations are required?

### Non-Functional Requirements
- [ ] Performance expectations?
- [ ] Scale requirements?
- [ ] Security/compliance requirements?
- [ ] Reliability/uptime expectations?

### Constraints
- [ ] Budget limitations?
- [ ] Timeline constraints?
- [ ] Technology mandates or restrictions?
- [ ] Organizational/political constraints?

---

## 4. Success Criteria

### Measurable Outcomes
- [ ] What metrics define success?
- [ ] What's the baseline for comparison?
- [ ] What's the target improvement?
- [ ] How will metrics be tracked?

### Qualitative Outcomes
- [ ] What should users feel/experience?
- [ ] What behaviors should change?
- [ ] What should become possible that isn't today?

### Timeframe
- [ ] When should value start appearing?
- [ ] What's the expected payback period?
- [ ] What milestones indicate progress?

---

## 5. Scope & Boundaries

### In Scope
- [ ] What's explicitly included?
- [ ] What use cases are covered?
- [ ] What user groups are served?

### Out of Scope
- [ ] What's explicitly excluded?
- [ ] What adjacent problems won't be solved?
- [ ] What future phases are deferred?

### Dependencies
- [ ] What must exist before this can succeed?
- [ ] What parallel efforts affect this?
- [ ] What downstream systems depend on this?

---

## 6. Risks & Mitigations

### Technical Risks
- [ ] What technical unknowns exist?
- [ ] What could fail technically?
- [ ] What skills/expertise might be lacking?

### Organizational Risks
- [ ] What change management challenges exist?
- [ ] What adoption barriers might arise?
- [ ] What political factors could derail this?

### Market/External Risks
- [ ] What external factors could impact success?
- [ ] What competitive dynamics matter?
- [ ] What regulatory considerations exist?

---

## 7. Context & Background

### Strategic Alignment
- [ ] Which ELT priority does this serve?
- [ ] How does this fit company strategy?
- [ ] What's the executive sponsor's vision?

### Historical Context
- [ ] What's been tried before?
- [ ] Why did previous attempts succeed/fail?
- [ ] What lessons should inform this effort?

### Related Initiatives
- [ ] What other projects intersect with this?
- [ ] Where might there be synergies?
- [ ] Where might there be conflicts?

---

## Coverage Assessment Guide

| Coverage Level | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Covered** | Clear, consistent answers from multiple sources |
| **Partial** | Some information but gaps or conflicts remain |
| **Missing** | No substantive information gathered yet |

### Minimum Viable Coverage for PRD
- Problem Definition: All items Covered
- Stakeholder Perspectives: Primary Users Covered, Secondary at least Partial
- Requirements: Functional Covered, Non-Functional at least Partial
- Success Criteria: Measurable Outcomes Covered
- Scope: In Scope and Out of Scope Covered
- Risks: At least Partial across all categories
- Context: Strategic Alignment Covered
