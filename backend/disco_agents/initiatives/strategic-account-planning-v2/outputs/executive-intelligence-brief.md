## The Headline

**Your AEs are spending 70-80% of their time gathering data instead of talking to customers.** The January 16th workshop confirmed this isn't a perception problem—it's a structural one. Data lives in 70-80 different places, there's no standard account plan format, and AI tools (when used) produce unreliable output that requires manual validation anyway.

**The good news:** You have everything you need to fix this. The workshop produced a gold standard template (Chris Powers' Nationwide white space map), clear process documentation, and a willing IT/AI team ready to build. The path to 70-80% automation is achievable in phases.

---

## What We Learned (That You Didn't Already Know)

### 1. Deep Research changes the game—but only if you use it right

Standard ChatGPT prompting produces "generic, unuseful responses" (Thomas). But Gemini Deep Research with well-crafted prompts gets you "99% accurate" starting points (Matt Lazar). The difference isn't the AI—it's the approach.

**Implication:** Training people to "prompt better" is the wrong investment. Build agent workflows that encode the right approach, so even new AEs get expert-level research.

### 2. Your best AEs have already solved this—you just haven't standardized it

Chris Powers' Nationwide white space map was universally praised. Matt Lazar's deep research process works. Thomas knows how to talk to AI naturally. These aren't skills to train—they're patterns to capture and replicate as automated workflows.

### 3. The CSM knowledge gap is bigger than you think

CSMs have critical internal footprint knowledge, but engagement is "Slack messages and informal conversations." No formal process exists to capture what they know. This is low-hanging fruit—a simple checklist would dramatically improve information flow.

### 4. Partner data is gatekept unnecessarily

AEs can't access Crossbeam directly. They have to "reach out to the partner team" for every lookup. This is friction that serves no one.

## The Recommendation

**Phase 1 (4 weeks): Foundation**
- Standardize the white space template (based on Nationwide example)
- Build a white space generation agent using Deep Research
- Create CSM engagement checklist
- Open Crossbeam read access to AEs

**Phase 2 (8 weeks): Integration**
- Build internal footprint aggregation (Salesforce + Tableau + Gatekeeper → single view)
- Add QA/validation layer to research agents
- Partner matching automation with Slack notifications

**Phase 3 (12 weeks): Intelligence**
- Strategic alignment agent (10K analysis, earnings reports)
- Pattern recognition across account types
- Continuous improvement based on usage data
