# Frequently Asked Questions

This page answers common questions about using Thesis and the SuperAssistant platform.

## Account & Access Questions

### How do I add a new user to the platform?

Adding new users is an **administrator-only** feature. If you need a colleague added to the platform:

1. Contact your organization's Thesis administrator
2. Provide them with the new user's email address and preferred name
3. The administrator will send an invitation from **Dashboard** → **Users** → **Add User**
4. The new user will receive an email invitation to set up their account

If you're unsure who your administrator is, check with your manager or IT department.

### How do I change my password or account settings?

Currently, password management is handled through the authentication system:

1. On the login page, click **Forgot Password**
2. Enter your email address
3. Follow the reset link sent to your email

For other account changes (name, email), contact your administrator.

### What's the difference between User and Admin roles?

| Feature | User | Admin |
|---------|------|-------|
| Chat with Thesis | Yes | Yes |
| Upload documents | Yes | Yes |
| Create projects | Yes | Yes |
| View My Impact dashboard | Yes | Yes |
| Access admin dashboard | No | Yes |
| Manage other users | No | Yes |
| View all conversations | No | Yes |
| Customize platform theme | No | Yes |
| Manage help documentation | No | Yes |

## Document & Upload Questions

### How do I upload documents?

To upload documents for Thesis to reference in your conversations:

1. Navigate to the **Chat** page
2. Look for the **paperclip icon** or **Upload** button in the chat input area
3. Click to open the file picker
4. Select your document (PDF, Word, PowerPoint, or text files)
5. Wait for the upload and processing to complete (you'll see a progress indicator)
6. Once processed, Thesis can reference this document in your conversations

**Supported formats:**
- PDF (.pdf)
- Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx)
- PowerPoint (.ppt, .pptx)
- Plain text (.txt)
- Markdown (.md)

### How long does document processing take?

- **Small documents** (1-10 pages): Usually 10-30 seconds
- **Medium documents** (10-50 pages): 30 seconds to 2 minutes
- **Large documents** (50+ pages): 2-5 minutes

You'll see a processing status indicator. Once complete, the document becomes searchable for Thesis to reference.

### Why isn't Thesis using my uploaded document?

If Thesis doesn't seem to reference your uploaded document:

1. **Check processing status** - Make sure the document finished processing (look for a success indicator)
2. **Be specific in your questions** - Reference content or topics from the document explicitly
3. **Wait a moment** - Very recently uploaded documents may take a few seconds to become searchable
4. **Try rephrasing** - Ask Thesis: "Based on the document I uploaded about [topic], can you..."

### Can I delete documents I've uploaded?

Yes, you can manage your uploaded documents:

1. Look for a **Documents** or **My Documents** section
2. Find the document you want to remove
3. Click the delete option

Note: Deleting a document means Thesis can no longer reference it in future conversations.

## Image Generation Questions

### How do I generate training images?

Simply describe what you need in your conversation with Thesis:

**Examples:**
- "Generate an image of a diverse team brainstorming in a modern office"
- "Create an image showing a manager providing feedback to an employee"
- "Show me a customer service representative helping a client"

Or use the command shortcut: `/image [your description]`

Thesis automatically detects image requests and generates them in 3-5 seconds.

### Can Thesis create infographics?

Thesis has limitations with complex infographics:

**What Thesis CAN do:**
- Generate simple visual scenes and scenarios
- Create images of people, workplaces, and environments
- Produce conceptual illustrations

**What Thesis CANNOT do:**
- Generate readable text within images
- Create data visualizations or charts
- Include logos or brand marks
- Produce highly technical diagrams

**For infographics**, we recommend:
- Use Thesis to generate supporting visuals
- Create the infographic structure in tools like Canva, PowerPoint, or Adobe Illustrator
- Combine Thesis's images with your text and data elements

### Why did my image generation fail?

Image generation may fail if:

1. **Content restrictions** - The request may have triggered safety filters
2. **Service temporarily busy** - Try again in a moment
3. **Unclear description** - Try being more specific about what you want

If problems persist, try rephrasing your request or contact support.

## Conversation & Chat Questions

### Where are my past conversations?

All your conversations are automatically saved:

1. Look for a **Conversations** or **Chat History** section
2. You'll see a list of your previous conversations
3. Click any conversation to continue where you left off

### Can I export my conversations?

Yes! To export a conversation:

1. Open the conversation you want to export
2. Look for an **Export** button or menu option
3. Choose your format:
   - **JSON** - Full data with metadata (good for records)
   - **TXT** - Plain text (good for reading/printing)

### How do I start a new conversation?

1. Navigate to the **Chat** page
2. Click **New Conversation** or the **+** button
3. Start typing your first message to Thesis

### Does Thesis remember previous conversations?

Thesis maintains context **within** a single conversation but starts fresh in new conversations.

**Within a conversation:** Thesis remembers everything discussed
**Across conversations:** Each new conversation starts fresh

To continue a previous project, open that conversation from your history rather than starting new.

## Projects & My Impact Questions

### What is the My Impact dashboard?

The **My Impact** dashboard shows your personal metrics based on the Bradbury Impact Loop methodology:

- **Ideation Velocity** - How quickly you generate and explore ideas
- **Correction Loop** - Your iterative refinement patterns
- **Overall engagement** with the platform

This helps you track your growth and effectiveness over time.

### What are Projects?

Projects help you organize related work:

- Group conversations around a specific initiative
- Track progress on learning design efforts
- Keep related documents together

### How do I create a new project?

1. Navigate to the **Projects** page
2. Click **New Project** or **+**
3. Give your project a name and optional description
4. Start adding conversations and documents to the project

## Troubleshooting

### Thesis isn't responding

If Thesis seems stuck:

1. **Wait a moment** - Complex requests may take longer
2. **Check your internet connection**
3. **Refresh the page**
4. **Try a shorter, simpler message**

If problems persist, the system may be experiencing temporary issues. Try again in a few minutes.

### I'm seeing an error message

Common errors and solutions:

| Error | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Session expired" | Refresh the page and log in again |
| "Upload failed" | Check file size (usually max 10-25MB) and format |
| "Service unavailable" | Wait a few minutes and try again |

### The page isn't loading correctly

Try these steps:

1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check if your organization's network allows access

### How do I get more help?

If you can't find your answer:

1. **Ask Thesis** - Use the help chat for platform questions
2. **Contact your administrator** - For account and access issues
3. **Check documentation** - Look for topic-specific guides in the help system
