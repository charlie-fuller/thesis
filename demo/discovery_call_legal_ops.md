# Discovery Call: Meridian Legal Ops Team
**Date:** January 22, 2026
**Duration:** 45 minutes
**Attendees:** Charlie Fuller, Lenora Chen (Director of Legal Operations), Maven Okonkwo (VP of Legal)

---

## Meeting Notes

**Introductions**

Charlie introduced himself and his background in operationalizing AI workflows. Explained interest in understanding how legal ops teams are thinking about agentic AI adoption.

Lenora has been Director of Legal Ops at Meridian for 3 years. Her team of 6 handles contract lifecycle management, compliance tracking, and legal intake for a 400-person company. Previously at a BigLaw firm where she saw "the worst of manual processes."

Maven joined as VP of Legal 18 months ago. Background in fintech compliance. Reports to the CEO and has been pushing for modernization of the legal function. Said "we're drowning in contracts and our outside counsel spend is unsustainable."

---

## Current Pain Points

**Contract Review Backlog**
- 200+ contracts in queue at any given time
- Average turnaround is 12 days, business teams want 3
- Lenora: "We're the bottleneck and everyone knows it"

**Compliance Monitoring**
- Track 14 different regulatory frameworks
- Currently using spreadsheets and calendar reminders
- Maven: "We had a near-miss on a GDPR deadline last quarter. Can't happen again."

**Knowledge Management**
- Tribal knowledge problem - only 2 people know the vendor agreement history
- No good way to search past contracts for precedent language
- Lenora mentioned they tried a CLM tool last year but "it was more work than it saved"

---

## Ideas They're Exploring

**1. AI-Assisted Contract Triage**
Lenora described wanting a system that could read incoming contracts, flag risk areas, and route to the right reviewer based on contract type and complexity. "I don't need AI to negotiate - I need it to tell me where to focus."

**2. Compliance Calendar Agent**
Maven brought up the idea of an agent that monitors regulatory changes and proactively alerts the team. Referenced a vendor called "RegWatch" but said pricing was "absurd for what it does."

**3. Contract Q&A Bot**
Lenora wants something where business users can ask "what's our liability cap with Vendor X" without filing a legal intake ticket. Estimated 40% of intake requests are just questions about existing agreements.
