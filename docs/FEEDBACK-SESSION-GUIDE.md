# DISCO v4.3 Feedback Session Guide

**Purpose:** Get Chris and Tyler's input on whether the DISCO pipeline outputs are actually useful before further iteration.

**Time Estimate:** 45-60 minutes total (can split across sessions)

**Initiative Used:** Strategic Account Planning

---

## How This Works

Review each stage in order. For each:
1. Read the output (2-3 min)
2. Answer the questions (3-5 min discussion)
3. Note any "this is wrong" or "this is missing" feedback

We're looking for alignment on whether the outputs help you do your jobs, not whether they're technically correct.

---

## Stage 1: Triage (5-10 min)

### Document to Review
**File:** Most recent Triage output for Strategic Account Planning
**Location:** In the UI under PuRDy > Strategic Account Planning > Triage

### What This Agent Does
Makes GO/NO-GO/INVESTIGATE decisions in <5 minutes. Added "Problem Worth Solving" gate in v4.2.

### Questions for Chris & Tyler

1. **The Problem Gate:** Does the 4-criteria table (real, costly, solvable, ours) match how you'd validate whether something is worth pursuing?
   - Missing criteria?
   - Wrong criteria?

2. **The Decision:** Is "GO with caveats" (proceed to discovery but capture baseline hours first) the right call here, or would you have decided differently?

3. **The Format:** Can you scan this in under 2 minutes and know what to do next?

4. **What's Missing:** What would you need to see that isn't here?

---

## Stage 2: Discovery Planner (5-10 min)

### Document to Review
**File:** Most recent Discovery Planner output
**Location:** In the UI under PuRDy > Strategic Account Planning > Discovery Planner

### What This Agent Does
Designs discovery sessions with agendas, attendees, and "done when" criteria.

### Questions for Chris & Tyler

1. **Session Design:** Would you actually run Session 1 (AE Time Quantification Workshop) as designed? What would you change?

2. **The Right Questions:** Are these the questions you'd ask to capture baseline hours?

3. **Attendee List:** Right people in the room?

4. **Done When:** Are the success criteria clear enough to know when you're finished?

---

## Stage 3: Insight Extractor (5-10 min)

### Document to Review
**File:** Most recent Insight Extractor output
**Location:** In the UI under PuRDy > Strategic Account Planning > Insight Extractor

### What This Agent Does
Distills transcripts into structured insights with evidence. Identifies patterns and contradictions.

### Questions for Chris & Tyler

1. **The Insights:** Do these 5 insights capture what actually matters from the discovery? Anything major missing?

2. **The Pattern Match:** It identified "The Governance Vacuum" pattern. Does that feel right, or is there a better framing?

3. **The Evidence:** Are the quotes compelling? Would they convince a skeptic?

4. **Surprises:** Did anything in the "What They Don't Realize" section actually surprise you?

---

## Stage 4: Consolidator (10 min) - MOST IMPORTANT

### Document to Review
**File:** Most recent Consolidator output
**Location:** In the UI under PuRDy > Strategic Account Planning > Consolidator

### What This Agent Does
Creates the decision document. This is what would go to Steve/Massimo.

### Questions for Chris & Tyler

1. **The 30-Second Test:** After 30 seconds of reading, can you state:
   - The decision? (GO/NO-GO)
   - The leverage point?
   - The first action?

2. **Would You Sign It:** Would you put your name on this and present it to Steve/Massimo as-is?
   - If no, what needs to change?

3. **The Metrics:** Does the Baseline → Target → Timeline table make sense? Are those the right KPIs?

4. **The System Diagram:** Does the reinforcing loop accurately represent the problem? Is the intervention point right?

5. **The Blockers:** Are these the real blockers? Missing any?

6. **Real Names:** We tried to use real names instead of "Discovery Lead." Did we get the right people? Is this format helpful or awkward?

---

## Stage 5: Strategist (10 min) - NEW

### Document to Review
**File:** Most recent Strategist output
**Location:** In the UI under PuRDy > Strategic Account Planning > Strategist

### What This Agent Does
Clusters insights into initiative bundles that humans can review, prioritize, and approve before PRD generation.

### Questions for Chris & Tyler

1. **The Bundles:** Does grouping these into 4 bundles (Governance, Prompt Library, Automation, Data Quality) make sense? Would you slice it differently?

2. **The Scores:** Are the Impact/Feasibility/Urgency ratings defensible? Any that feel wrong?

3. **The Dependencies:** Does the "Bundle 1 blocks everything else" framing match reality?

4. **Human Checkpoint:** Is this the right level of detail for you to review and approve before we generate PRDs? Too much? Too little?

5. **Prioritization:** Do you agree with the recommended sequencing (Governance first, then Prompt Library, then Automation)?

---

## Stage 6: PRD Generator (10 min) - NEW

### Document to Review
**File:** Most recent PRD Generator output
**Location:** In the UI under PuRDy > Strategic Account Planning > PRD Generator

### What This Agent Does
Transforms an approved bundle into an engineering-ready PRD.

### Questions for Chris & Tyler

1. **Engineering Readiness:** Could you hand this to an engineering team and expect them to start planning?
   - If no, what's missing?

2. **Requirements Table:** Are the functional requirements specific enough? Too vague? Too detailed?

3. **Success Metrics:** Are baseline → target → timeline realistic and measurable?

4. **Scope Clarity:** Is Out of Scope clear? Would this prevent scope creep?

5. **The Format:** Is this the PRD structure you'd use, or do you have a different template you prefer?

6. **Traceability:** Does linking requirements back to discovery quotes add value, or is it noise?

---

## Wrap-Up Questions (5 min)

1. **Overall Flow:** Does Discovery → Insights → Synthesis → Convergence make sense as a pipeline? Any stages that feel unnecessary or missing?

2. **Human Checkpoints:** Are checkpoints after Consolidator and Strategist the right places to pause for human review?

3. **What Would Make This Useful:** If you could change one thing about the whole pipeline to make it actually useful for your work, what would it be?

4. **Ship or Iterate:** Based on what you've seen, would you use this pipeline on the next initiative, or does it need more work first?
