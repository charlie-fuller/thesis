# Thesis Platform Overview

## What Is Thesis?

Thesis is an AI-powered strategy assistant that helps enterprise teams plan and execute GenAI initiatives. Instead of a single chatbot, Thesis provides a team of 21 specialized AI agents, each with expertise in a specific domain.

## Core Concept

**One platform, many expert perspectives.**

When planning GenAI strategy, organizations need input from multiple viewpoints: finance, legal, IT security, change management, research, and more. Thesis simulates this cross-functional collaboration by letting you consult domain-specific AI agents or bring them together in virtual meeting rooms.

## The Three Pillars

### 1. Expert Agents
Specialized AI advisors covering:
- Business (Finance, Legal, Strategy, Operations)
- Technical (IT/Security, Architecture, Research)
- People (Change Management, Training, Communications)

### 2. Knowledge Base
A centralized repository where your documents become the foundation for AI responses. Agents cite your materials, not just general knowledge.

### 3. Collaboration Spaces
Meeting rooms where multiple agents discuss topics together, offering diverse perspectives in a single conversation.

## Who Is It For?

Enterprise teams evaluating, planning, or implementing GenAI initiatives who need structured guidance across multiple domains without hiring a full consulting team.

## The Value Proposition

Transform fragmented GenAI planning into coordinated strategy with AI experts that understand your context, cite your documents, and collaborate across disciplines.
