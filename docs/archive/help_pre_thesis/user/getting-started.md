# Getting Started with Thesis

Welcome to Thesis! This guide will help you understand how to use Thesis effectively for designing ROI-driven L&D programs.

## What Can Thesis Do?

Thesis is an AI-powered assistant specifically designed for L&D (Learning & Development) professionals. Here's everything you can do with Thesis:

### Design & Create

- **Learning programs** - Design complete training curricula using the DDLD framework
- **Assessments** - Create evaluations that measure real skill application, not just recall
- **Facilitator guides** - Generate professional guides for trainers and facilitators
- **Training visuals** - Generate images for slides, handouts, and learning materials
- **ROI measurement plans** - Design plans to prove training impact

### Analyze & Advise

- **Evaluate programs** - Get analysis of existing training programs using learning science
- **Strategic guidance** - Receive advice on L&D initiatives and stakeholder management
- **Gap analysis** - Identify knowledge and skill gaps causing performance problems

### Work with Your Content

- **Upload documents** - Add your organization's L&D materials for context
- **Reference your content** - Thesis uses your uploaded documents in responses
- **Maintain alignment** - Ensure outputs match your company's approach and terminology

## Core Concepts

### The DDLD Framework

Thesis's primary methodology for designing effective training:

1. **Data** - What measurable business problem needs solving? (sales conversion rate, customer satisfaction scores, error rates)
2. **Desired state** - What does success look like in numbers? (increase conversion by 15%, reduce errors by 30%)
3. **Learning gap** - What specific knowledge/skills gap is causing the problem?
4. **Difference** - How will we measure that learning transferred to on-the-job performance?

### Operating Modes

Thesis can operate in four different modes depending on your needs:

- **Coach** - Collaborative partner for brainstorming and refining ideas
- **Developer** - Creates polished, client-ready deliverables (curricula, assessments, facilitator guides)
- **Analyst** - Evaluates existing programs and identifies gaps using learning science
- **Advisor** - Provides strategic guidance on L&D initiatives and stakeholder management

## How to Talk to Thesis

### Natural Conversation

Just talk naturally! Thesis understands context and can handle complex requests:

**Examples:**
- "I need to design a training program for new sales managers who are struggling with pipeline management"
- "Create an assessment that measures whether learners can actually apply consultative selling techniques"
- "Generate an image of a diverse team collaborating in a modern office for my leadership training slides"

### Be Specific About What You Need

The more context you provide, the better Thesis can help:

**Good:**
"Create a 2-day facilitator guide for customer service training. The target audience is frontline retail staff. Focus on de-escalation techniques and empathy. Include activities that use real customer scenarios from our company."

**Less helpful:**
"I need training materials"

## Key Features

### 1. Document Upload

Upload your organization's L&D documents to give Thesis context about your work.

**How to upload:**

1. Navigate to the **Chat** page
2. Look for the **paperclip icon** or **Upload** button near the chat input
3. Click to open the file picker
4. Select your document
5. Wait for processing to complete (10 seconds to a few minutes depending on size)

**Supported formats:**
- PDF (.pdf)
- Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx)
- PowerPoint (.ppt, .pptx)
- Plain text (.txt)

**What to upload:**
- Existing training programs
- Company values and culture documents
- Industry-specific terminology guides
- Approved methodologies and frameworks

Once uploaded, Thesis will reference these documents when providing guidance, ensuring alignment with your organization's approach.

### 2. Image Generation

Thesis can create training visuals in 3-5 seconds using AI:

**What you can generate:**
- Scenario backgrounds (office, retail, healthcare settings)
- Concept visualizations (leadership, teamwork, growth mindsets)
- Diverse characters for inclusive representation
- Process diagrams and job aids
- Presentation graphics

**How to request:**
Just ask naturally - Thesis automatically detects image requests:
- "Generate an image of a manager giving constructive feedback"
- "Show me a diverse team in a modern collaborative workspace"
- "Create a landscape image showing a customer service representative helping a client"

### 3. Command Shortcuts

Power users can use shortcuts for common tasks:

- `/assess` - Create assessments
- `/roi` - Design ROI measurement plan
- `/outline` - Generate program outline
- `/image` - Generate training visual

### 4. Projects and Conversation History

- Organize conversations into projects
- View all conversations in expandable lists within each project
- Start new chats directly from any project using the **+** button
- Search and resume past conversations
- Export conversations for documentation

### 5. My Impact Dashboard

Track your productivity and learning design growth with personal analytics:

- **Activity Summary** - Conversations, messages, documents, and images over time
- **Design Velocity** - How many days you've worked on learning design projects
- **Systems Thinking Score** - Quality assessment across 6 learning design dimensions

Access via your user menu (name/avatar) → **My Impact**. Choose from Week, Month, or All Time views.
