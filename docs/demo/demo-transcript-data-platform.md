## Participants

## Summary
Data leadership alignment meeting to approve the Unified Analytics Hub initiative. Strong consensus on the business need to consolidate fragmented data sources and enable self-service analytics powered by AI.

## Key Discussion Points

### Unified Analytics Hub Initiative

Priya Sharma outlined the vision for a centralized analytics platform that uses AI to democratize data access. Currently, data requests take 2-3 weeks through the analytics team backlog.

**Strategic Business Case:**
- **ROI Potential**: Very High - estimated 60% reduction in time-to-insight, $500K/year in analyst efficiency
- **Effort Level**: High - 4 sprints for MVP with natural language query interface
- **Strategic Alignment**: CEO priority - directly supports data-driven decision making goal
- **Budget**: Approved - $300K from data platform budget
- **Champion**: Priya Sharma (VP of Data Science)

Tom Anderson confirmed his team is overwhelmed with ad-hoc requests and fully supports this initiative. Michelle Rodriguez will represent the product org as primary user advocate.

**Decision**: Approved as Tier-1 priority. MVP target is end of Q1.
