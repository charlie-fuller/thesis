## Key Discussion Points

### AI Model Governance Framework

Marcus Chen presented the need for a comprehensive governance framework to manage AI model deployments across the organization. This addresses both security risks and upcoming regulatory requirements.

**Strategic Business Case:**
- **ROI Potential**: High - avoids potential $2M+ in regulatory fines and reputational damage
- **Effort Level**: Medium - 3 sprints for core framework, ongoing maintenance
- **Strategic Alignment**: Board mandate - risk committee flagged AI governance as critical gap
- **Budget**: Approved - $200K from security budget plus $50K from legal
- **Champion**: Marcus Chen (CISO)

Jennifer Walsh confirmed the EU AI Act deadlines make this urgent. David Okonkwo will lead the technical implementation.

**Decision**: Approved as Tier-1 priority. Must be operational before Q2 regulatory audit.
