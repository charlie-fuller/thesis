## Key Discussion Points

### Customer Intelligence Platform Initiative

Chris Baumgartner emphasized this as a Tier-1 strategic priority for Q1. The initiative would use AI to aggregate customer feedback from support tickets, NPS surveys, and product usage data to enable proactive intervention.

**Strategic Business Case:**
- **ROI Potential**: High - estimated 40% reduction in churn through proactive outreach
- **Effort Level**: Medium - approximately 2 sprints for MVP
- **Strategic Alignment**: CEO priority - directly supports company goal of improving customer retention by 25%
- **Budget**: Approved - $150K allocated from Q1 innovation budget
- **Champion**: Lisa Park (Head of Customer Success)

Rachel Thompson confirmed her team has the capacity to begin immediately.

**Decision**: Approved as Tier-1 priority with immediate start.
