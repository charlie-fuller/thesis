# Thesis Demo Notes - Finance All-Hands

**Date:** January 21, 2026
**Audience:** Finance team all-hands meeting

## Context

Potential demo opportunity to showcase Thesis platform. Goal is to demonstrate value and business impact rather than just walking through features.

---

## Recommended Demo Arc: "From Meeting to Action in Minutes"

### 1. The Problem (30 seconds)

Open with the pain point:

> "After client meetings, insights get lost in transcripts nobody re-reads. Action items slip. Stakeholder concerns get forgotten. Context rebuilding wastes hours."

### 2. The Magic Moment (2-3 minutes)

Upload a real Granola transcript and show the **cascade of intelligence**:

- Stakeholder candidates auto-extracted with sentiment
- Task candidates discovered and staged for review
- Opportunity candidates identified with tier scoring

This is the "wow" moment - one document triggers a pipeline of actionable intelligence.

### 3. The Multi-Agent Differentiator (2 minutes)

Open a meeting room with Capital (Finance), Guardian (IT), and Counselor (Legal). Ask a question like:

> "What are the risks of this AI initiative?"

Show how each agent brings their **stakeholder lens** - this isn't generic AI, it's simulating the actual perspectives around the table.

### 4. The Operator Context (1 minute)

Show the opportunities pipeline with tier scoring. Demonstrate how Operator auto-injects project triage context - the platform learns and surfaces relevant history.

### 5. Close with the "So What"

- "Every meeting becomes institutional memory"
- "Every stakeholder concern gets tracked, not forgotten"
- "Every opportunity gets scored consistently"

---

## Tips for Finance Audience

- Emphasize **time-to-insight** (minutes vs. hours of manual work)
- Mention Capital agent is modeled on Raul's perspective
- Show the ROI scoring dimensions on opportunities
- If possible, use a real (anonymized) client example

## Key Value Propositions

| Feature | Business Value |
|---------|----------------|
| Meeting transcript processing | Turns 60-min meetings into actionable items in minutes |
| Stakeholder auto-discovery | Never lose track of who said what or their concerns |
| Multi-agent perspectives | Get Finance, Legal, IT, People views without scheduling meetings |
| Opportunity pipeline | Consistent scoring framework for AI initiative prioritization |
| Knowledge base RAG | Instant recall of all prior context and documents |
