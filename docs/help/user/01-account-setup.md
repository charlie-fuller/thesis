# Account Setup & Profile Management

Customize your Thesis account to match your preferences and professional identity.

## Accessing Your Profile

Click your **avatar** or **name** in the top-right corner of the screen, then select **Profile Settings**.

Alternatively, navigate directly to `/profile` in your browser.

## Profile Settings

### Display Name

Your name appears in conversations and is used when exporting work. To update it:

1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Edit the **Name** field
3. Click **Save Changes**

### Profile Picture (Avatar)

Add a professional photo to personalize your account:

1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Click **Change Avatar** or the camera icon on your current avatar
3. Select an image file (JPG, PNG, or GIF)
4. Maximum file size: **5 MB**
5. Your new avatar appears immediately across the platform

**To remove your avatar:**
Click the **Remove** button next to your current avatar to revert to the default.

### Email Address

Your email is displayed in Profile Settings but cannot be changed directly. Contact your administrator if you need to update your email address.

## Password Management

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Scroll to the **Password** section
3. Enter your **Current Password**
4. Enter your **New Password**
5. Confirm by entering the new password again
6. Click **Update Password**

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- Recommended: Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols

### Forgot Your Password?

If you're locked out:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for a reset link
5. Follow the link to create a new password

## Account Information

Your profile displays read-only information about your account:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Role** | Your access level (User or Admin) |
| **Client ID** | Your organization identifier |
| **Email** | Your registered email address |

These fields are managed by your administrator and cannot be changed from your profile.

## View Mode (Admins Only)

If you have admin privileges, you'll see a **View Mode** toggle in the user menu:

- **Admin View** - Full access to admin dashboard, user management, and system settings
- **User View** - See the platform as regular users experience it

This is useful for testing and understanding the user experience without changing your actual permissions.

## Signing Out

To sign out of Thesis:

1. Click your avatar or name in the top-right corner
2. Select **Sign Out**

You'll be redirected to the login page. Your conversations and documents are saved and will be available when you sign back in.

## Tips for Your Profile

- **Use a professional photo** - It helps build rapport in team environments
- **Keep your name consistent** - Use the same name format across your organization's tools
- **Update your password regularly** - Security best practice, especially for accounts with document access
- **Check your role** - Understanding your access level helps you know what features are available
