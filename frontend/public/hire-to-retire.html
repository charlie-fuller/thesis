How to Use These Maps

1
Click any step card to expand it and see the full description, system details, HR key controls, and any CKO workshop flags associated with that step.
2
Red dot indicators mark steps that have CKO workshop flags -- pain points, manual processes, rework, or errors identified on Feb 12. A number means multiple flags.
3
Use the filter buttons to trace a specific system (like Workday) across all steps, or filter to "Flags Only" to see just the pain points for prioritization.
4
Circled letters (A, B, C) are cross-lane references. They show where a process hands off from one swim lane to another -- matching letters connect.
