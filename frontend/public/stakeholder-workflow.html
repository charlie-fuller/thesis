Continuous Stakeholder Engagement
Workflow Phases
1
Identify
Find stakeholders relevant to an initiative
  • Create profiles with name, role, department
  • Detect stakeholder mentions in KB documents
  • Link stakeholders to DISCO initiatives
  • Navigator agent can suggest stakeholders from document content
2
Assess
Evaluate each stakeholder's position
Sentiment Spectrum
Champion
Supporter
Neutral
Skeptic
Blocker
Influence Levels

  • Department and organizational position mapping
  • Relationship to initiative outcomes
3
Engage
Plan and execute engagement
  • Meeting rooms for stakeholder-focused discussions
  • Agent-prepared talking points and briefing materials
  • Link meeting transcripts to stakeholder records
  • Track engagement frequency and quality
4
Track
Monitor engagement over time
  • Engagement level history and trends
  • Meeting notes linked to stakeholders
  • Sentiment changes across initiative lifecycle
  • Cross-initiative stakeholder views
5
Adjust
Adapt strategy based on tracking data
  • Identify at-risk stakeholders (sentiment declining)
  • Re-prioritize engagement based on initiative needs
  • Agent insights on stakeholder dynamics
  • Prepare for difficult conversations
6
Re-engage
Close the loop
  • Schedule follow-ups based on tracking gaps
  • Share outcomes and progress updates
  • Convert blockers to supporters through targeted engagement
  • Feed relationship data back into future initiatives
Platform Integrations
DISCO Initiative Integration

Stakeholders are linked directly to DISCO initiatives, providing context for every phase of product discovery.

  • Stakeholder profiles attached to initiatives
  • Discovery phase identifies relevant stakeholders from KB documents
  • Intelligence phase analyzes stakeholder sentiment signals
  • Synthesis phase incorporates stakeholder perspectives into recommendations
  • Convergence phase maps stakeholder alignment to decision criteria
Projects Project Sponsorship

Stakeholders serve as project sponsors, champions, and key contacts throughout the project lifecycle.

  • Assign stakeholders as project sponsors or leads
  • Track sponsor engagement across project milestones
  • Surface stakeholder concerns in project health checks
  • Link project outcomes back to stakeholder expectations
  • Cross-project stakeholder view for portfolio management
Agents AI Agent Support

Specialized agents provide analysis and recommendations for stakeholder engagement strategy.

  • Navigator -- stakeholder mapping and analysis from document content
  • Catalyst -- change management strategies for stakeholder buy-in
  • Meeting room agents prepare stakeholder-specific talking points
  • Agents detect stakeholder mentions across KB documents and meetings
  • Automated engagement gap alerts when stakeholders go cold
